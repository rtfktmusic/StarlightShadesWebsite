Child Safety

Child Safety & Blind Cord Safety Information

All installations by Starlight Shades are in accordance with Australian standards

Window coverings with cords, chains, or loops can pose a serious strangulation risk to young children. At Starlight Shades, the safety of your family is our highest priority. We take every precaution to ensure our products meet or exceed Australian safety standards, and we're committed to helping you make informed choices about the window furnishings in your home.

ACCC Product Safety Australia recommends the following guidelines for keeping your home safe:

  • Walk through every room in your home and check for any blinds or curtains with long cords that are within reach of children. Pay special attention to bedrooms, playrooms, and living areas where young children spend most of their time.
  • Never place cots, beds, highchairs, or playpens near windows where children could reach blind cords or chains. Even short cords can become dangerous if a child climbs nearby furniture to access them.
  • Choose cordless blind options wherever possible — including spring-loaded rollers, motorised blinds, or wand-operated vertical blinds. These designs eliminate exposed cords entirely and are the safest choice for homes with children.
  • If your existing blinds have cords, ensure they are fitted with cord cleats or cord tensioners. These devices secure loose cords to the wall, keeping them taut and out of reach of little hands.
  • Always supervise children in rooms with corded window coverings. Even with safety devices installed, supervision remains the most effective way to prevent accidents.

From 1 January 2015, a new national mandatory standard applies to the installation of internal blinds, curtains, and window fittings across Australia. This standard requires all corded products to include safety devices that restrict access to cords.

A small adhesive label (30mm x 30mm) with the installation company's details is to be placed on or adjacent to a mounting bracket at the top of the blind, curtain, or window covering. Additionally, a safety warning sticker and cord safety clip are included with every corded product we install. Your installer will show you the location of these labels and safety clips, and explain how to use them properly.

For more information, visit the ACCC Product Safety Australia website or refer to the AS/NZS 13120 Cord Safety Standard.

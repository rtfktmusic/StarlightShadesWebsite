Automation

Introducing Home Automation – the perfect answer for contemporary lifestyles.

We are reinventing air, movement and comfort. Switching a controllable by the touch of a button, so why not blinds too? On the way home from work a simple touch of your smartphone or tablet can open or close your blinds, put as a premium on the lights, heater or air conditioner. The feature we once attributed to the rich and famous – motorisation and home automation have become an affordable addition to new and old homes of all scales.

Why choose Home Automation?

Convenience

Control all your window coverings at the touch of a button from your smartphone, tablet, or wall switch. Schedule your blinds to open and close automatically at the times that suit your lifestyle.

Energy Efficiency

Automated blinds can be programmed to respond to sunlight, temperature, and time of day, maximising insulation and reducing your reliance on heating and cooling systems throughout the year.

Security & Privacy

Set your blinds to open and close on a schedule while you are away, giving the impression the home is occupied. Enhance your home security without compromising on style or comfort.

Child & Pet Safety

Motorised blinds eliminate the need for cords and chains, creating a completely safe environment for children and pets. Enjoy complete peace of mind with a cord-free window covering solution.

Stay Sustainable

Our automated blinds play a significant role in reducing your home's energy consumption. By intelligently managing sunlight and heat gain, they help maintain a comfortable indoor temperature naturally, reducing the load on your heating and cooling systems.

Pair your motorised blinds with solar-powered charging accessories to further reduce your environmental footprint. Smart scheduling ensures your home is always at its most efficient, whether you are home or away.

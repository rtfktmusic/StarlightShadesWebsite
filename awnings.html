Awnings

Introducing Awnings – the outdoor solution for any window.

Enhance your home's curb appeal and energy efficiency with our stylish window awnings. Designed to provide shade and reduce glare, our awnings protect your interiors from harmful UV rays while keeping your home cooler in summer & warmer in winter. Available in a variety of styles, colours, and materials, our window awnings add both functionality and aesthetic charm, making them a perfect addition to any home.

View Gallery

Why choose Awnings?

Cost Saving Solution

Awnings significantly reduce heat gain and lower your air conditioning costs — a smart investment that pays for itself over time.

Endless Style

Choose from an extensive range of styles, fabrics, and colours tailored to complement any architectural style, traditional or contemporary.

Enhanced Privacy

Awnings create a natural visual barrier between your outdoor spaces and the street, giving you freedom and peace of mind.

Increased Living Area

Transform patios, decks, and alfresco areas into comfortable year-round living spaces, effectively extending your home.

Discover Your Choices

Style

Choose from straight drop, pivot arm, retractable, or folding arm awnings to suit your specific outdoor space and requirements.

Custom sizing available to fit any window or outdoor area.

Colour

Select from our extensive colour palette to match your home's exterior, garden, and personal style preferences perfectly.

Solid, striped, and pattern options available.

Operation

Manual crank, spring-loaded, or motorised operation available. Motorised systems can include wind sensors for added protection.

Smart home integration available on motorised models.

Expert In-Home Consultations

Our in-home consultation service takes the hassle out of buying new awnings. We'll bring fabric samples to you so you can see how they look in your space. Our advisors provide bespoke guidance, ensuring you make an informed decision you'll love.
